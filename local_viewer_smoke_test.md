# Local viewer smoke test

The updated repository `index.html` was served locally at `http://127.0.0.1:8080/index.html` and opened in Chromium. The MapLibre viewer loaded with the title **prettymaps places, rendered with converted OpenFreeMap styles**, the place selector, style selector, navigation buttons, and the full list of showcase places.

The visible places included Stad van de Zon, Stad van de Zon minimal preset, Praça Ferreira do Amaral, Bom Fim, Centro Histórico, Barcelona, Barcelona plotter variant, Barra da Tijuca, Cidade Baixa, Farroupilha, Honolulu, and Garopaba. The browser console showed no errors after page load.
