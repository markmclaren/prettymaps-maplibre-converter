prettymaps places, rendered with converted OpenFreeMap styles

This viewer visits the example places used in the prettymaps notebook and applies the converted MapLibre styles created from the prettymaps presets, plus a Lè Shine-inspired blue-globe style.

Serve this folder over HTTP, then open index.html. Use the Texture selector to compare flat and halftone styles. Example: python3.11 -m http.server 8080.
